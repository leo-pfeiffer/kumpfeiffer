{% trans "Leo and Kristina met on Wednesday, during their first week in St Andrews. Called ‘Sinners’ it is a bi-weekly night out for the various sports teams and societies at St Andrews. Kristina was coming back from Pres hosted by the Dance team, and Leo was on a pub crawl with his flatmates from Gannochy, one of whom Kristina happened to have met earlier that week. She decided to tag along as the group went into a local bar and restaurant The Saint. After chatting with her friend for a bit she looked over and noticed Leo, enjoying a slice of cake.
